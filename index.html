Biography

Who is Hev Abi? Hev Abi is a rapper and singer that is well known in the Pinoy Music Industry. He came from Philippines, Quezon City Tomas Morato. He is well known all over social media and has monthly listeners of 5,765,435 according to Spotify PH. With him being on billboards in Mandaluyong and appears in other ads or videos his face and his music is iconic.
